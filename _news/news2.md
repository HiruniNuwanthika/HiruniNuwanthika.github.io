Invited to speak on the panel "Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration: Bringing Social Science and Computational Analysis Together" at SICSS-Melbourne; a winter school on computational social science held at RMIT University. 

The session brought together perspectives from computer science and the social sciences to discuss the opportunities and challenges of interdisciplinary research, drawing on my experience integrating value sensitive design and human-centred methods within computational research.
