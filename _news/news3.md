Attended UbiComp/ISWC, held at Aalto University and  presented the paper "Watch Out! E-scooter Coming Through!: Multimodal Sensing of Mixed Traffic Use and Conflicts Through Riders' Ego-centric Views". The paper investigates rider behaviour across three transport infrastructures using eye-tracking, camera, and speed data collected under real-world conditions, with the accompanying dataset and analysis code made openly available. Alongside presenting, I served as a student volunteer and co-organised the UbiComp4VRU workshop on ubiquitous computing for vulnerable road users, and had the opportunity to connect with researchers from across the field.
