Presented our paper "Applying Value Sensitive Design to Location-Based Services” at CHI-M 2026 organised by SIGCHI Melbourne.

The paper explores how Location-Based Services (LBS) such as ride sharing, accommodation platforms, food delivery, and micro-mobility sharing are embedded in the physical spaces where they operate, shaping interactions among multiple stakeholders. While these systems are often designed with direct stakeholders in mind, their impacts extend to indirect stakeholders.

📍 In this work, introduce Location-Aware Value Sensitive Design (LA-VSD), a domain-specific adaptation of Value Sensitive Design (VSD) tailored to the distinctive characteristics of LBS. 

🛴 We demonstrate the framework through a case study of e-scooter sharing.

This work was conducted as part of my PhD.
