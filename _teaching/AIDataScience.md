Conducted workshops and marked assignments for this subject examining the professional, ethical, and societal dimensions of AI and data science practice. Topics spanned legal, privacy, and governance considerations in data management; responsible and ethical AI practice; the broader social and economic impacts of intelligent systems; and effective communication of analyses to inform organisational decision-making.
