Tutored and marked assignments for this subject covering the theory and practice of usability engineering across the user-centred design lifecycle. Topics spanned user research techniques, qualitative and statistical analysis in usability testing, experimental design, heuristic evaluation, large-scale and industrial usability testing, and designing for diverse user groups across accessibility and cultural considerations.
